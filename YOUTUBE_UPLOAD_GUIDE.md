# YouTube Upload Guide - Enable Embedding

Follow these steps to upload your video to YouTube with embedding enabled:

## Step-by-Step Instructions:

### 1. Go to YouTube Studio
- Visit: https://studio.youtube.com
- Sign in with your Google account

### 2. Upload Your Video
- Click the **"Create"** button (top right)
- Select **"Upload videos"**
- Choose your plot video file
- Wait for upload to complete

### 3. **IMPORTANT: Enable Embedding** ⚠️
While the video is uploading or after it's uploaded:

1. Go to **"Details"** tab (or click "SHOW MORE" if needed)
2. Scroll down to **"Distribution"** section
3. Look for **"Allow embedding"** checkbox
4. ✅ **CHECK THIS BOX** - This is CRITICAL!
5. Make sure video is set to **"Public"** or **"Unlisted"** (not Private)

### 4. Get Your Video Link
- After publishing, copy the video URL
- It will look like: `https://youtu.be/VIDEO_ID` or `https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID`
- Copy the VIDEO_ID (the part after `v=` or after `youtu.be/`)

### 5. Update Your Website
- Once you have the new video ID, I'll help you update the code
- Just share the new YouTube link with me

## Common Issues:
- **Error 153**: Embedding is NOT enabled - go back and check the box
- **Video not showing**: Make sure video is Public/Unlisted, not Private
- **Still not working**: Clear browser cache and try again
